Empowering Women in Tech: An Inspiring Panel Discussion

Join us for an empowering event where three distinguished women share their journeys to success in the tech industry, a field traditionally dominated by men. Their stories are not about luck but about hard work, perseverance, and the power of self-promotion. As Liz Rogers puts it, "I learned very early on that if I did not promote myself - no one else was going to."

Meet Our Panelists:
Liz Rogers

Liz Rogers serves as the Chief Information Officer for Lexington-Fayette Urban County Government. With over 20 years of experience in Kentucky state government, Liz has led diverse IT teams and initiatives across multiple disciplines. Her career is characterized by a dedication to service management, continuous improvement, and her unique ability to integrate people, processes, products, and partners to enhance governmental technology services. Liz is a lifelong resident of the Bluegrass region and holds a bachelor's degree in Telecommunications from the University of Kentucky.

Amanda Sarratore

Amanda Sarratore, Chief Information Officer at The Lexington School, is renowned for her strategic planning, network security readiness, and IT operational excellence. With a career marked by successful leadership of high-performing teams and the delivery of over 7,500 hours of IT projects, Amanda balances the school's IT needs with its strategic goals. She holds an Executive MBA from the University of Notre Dame and a BS in Management from the University of Tennessee at Martin, along with certifications in Project Management Professional (PMP), Six Sigma, and ITIL Foundations.

Smitha Chopra

Smitha Chopra is the Director of Product Development & Integration at the University of Kentucky’s Information Technology Services (ITS) - Enterprise Applications. She excels in collaborating with campus leaders to drive impactful digital products that enhance user experiences, create efficiencies, and support digital transformation objectives. With a strong background in enterprise-scale software development, Smitha leads a team of talented software designers, architects, and engineers. A resident of Lexington, KY, she enjoys exploring new travel and culinary adventures with her family and friends.

Come and be inspired by these trailblazing women as they discuss their paths to leadership, the challenges they’ve overcome, and the strategies they’ve used to succeed in a competitive industry. Don't miss this opportunity to learn from their experiences and gain insights on advancing your career in technology.
