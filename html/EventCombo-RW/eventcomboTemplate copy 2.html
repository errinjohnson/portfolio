Harnessing Data-Driven Decisions | Power BI 101 for Product Managers and Operations

Join us for an insightful session on how data-driven decisions can transform your organization. This Virtual Lunch and Learn will delve into the powerful capabilities of Power BI and how it can be utilized by product managers and operations teams to make informed decisions that drive success.

In today's fast-paced business environment, making data-driven decisions is essential across all roles and industries. Whether you are managing products, overseeing operations, or involved in strategic planning, leveraging data can enhance your effectiveness and outcomes. This introductory presentation will explore practical applications and real-world examples of how Power BI can provide actionable insights and streamline decision-making processes - no experience with Power BI necessary to join!

Learn how to harness the full potential of your data, uncover hidden trends, and make strategic decisions that propel your organization forward. This session is designed for professionals looking to enhance their data analytics skills and apply them to a variety of roles within their organizations.

Caitlin Cambron

Caitlin has 9 years of experience at technology startups, where she has been a UX Designer, Product Manager, people leader, and operational efficiency enthusiast. She has launched 10+ digital products and managed $2M in revenue. Most notably, she has designed complex healthcare systems and launched GoodMaps, an innovative indoor navigation app that empowers people with disabilities. The 2023 HCII conference featured her research on how blind students navigate, reflecting her dedication to accessible design. Caitlin enjoys telling stories with data to support informed strategic decisions.

Caitlin holds degrees in Visual Communication and Spanish Literature and Linguistics from Ball State University, where she graduated with honors. In 2019, she was a guest professor at the University of Louisville, teaching a 2-day mini-course on UX Design. That same year, her contributions to product design were recognized when she was named "Technology's Rising Star" by Today's Woman.

Beyond her professional endeavors, Caitlin volunteers as the Chief of Staff for RockIT Women, focusing on the organization's strategic outlook and operations.
